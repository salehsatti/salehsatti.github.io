My research focuses on the confluence of climate, water and societies. I assess ways to improve seasonal precipitation forecasts, and try to better understand how these forecasts affect water resources management. Ultimately my goal is to add to the growing literature within the climate and water communities aimed at building resilience to hydroclimatic extremes. I am currently a fourth year PhD student at Johns Hopkins University in the department of Earth and Planetary Sciences working under Dr. Benjamin Zaitchik.
